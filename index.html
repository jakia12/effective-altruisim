How $EA works

Use Evidence to Find Solutions

Effective Altruists look for interventions and actions backed by data and research.This involves evaluating evidence-based solutions to problems, often drawing from fields like economics, public health, and philosophy

Consider Existential Risks

EA doesn’t only focus on immediate issues but also considers future generations. This includes addressing existential risks like climate change, nuclear war, and AI alignment. EA aims to prevent catastrophes that could drastically reduce humanity’s potential.

Promote a Global Perspective

Effective Altruists value the welfare of all individuals equally, regardless of nationality, race, or species. This often leads to a focus on areas like global poverty, animal welfare, and the rights of future generations, recognizing that everyone’s well-being matters.
